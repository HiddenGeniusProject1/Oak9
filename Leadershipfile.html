What is Leadership?:
In the Hidden Genius Project, on our first day, we deeply discussed the idea of leadership. To me, leadership is carrying a community and always being the one that takes the first step in any situations, making others then feel comfortable and follow their lead. Leadership is also the idea of helping out in different communities and making sure that the people they're working with are ready to pitch in.
